[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
I got a pocket full of moonlight just for you
And a quiet little corner where the shadows fall in two
So come a little closer to the firelight glow
'Cause underneath the stetson, cowboy tears don't show

[Verse 1]
The midnight highway's been my only friend
But I'd trade every mile just to hold your hand
Your whiskey-colored eyes got me feeling so weak
And every time you look at me, it's hard to speak

[Pre-Chorus]
There's a storm brewing up in my chest tonight
But your sweet perfume makes it all feel right
I'm throwing down my guard when the lights burn low
I want you more than you'll ever know

[Chorus]
Oh, the radio is humming a slow-burn tune
And I'm tangled in the magic of the southern moon
I'm a hard-traveling man with a reckless soul
But tonight with you, cowboy tears don't show

[Post-Chorus]
Let the shadows fall down, let the wild wind blow
[Ad-lib] (Cowboy tears don't show, no they never show)

[Verse 2]
I've been a solitary shadow in a crowded room
But your velvet touch is melting all the gloom
You're sliding off your jacket in the candle's flame
And whispering my secrets like a holy name

[Pre-Chorus 2]
There's a storm brewing up in my chest tonight
But your sweet perfume makes it all feel right

[Chorus]

But tonight with you, cowboy tears don't show

[Post-Chorus 2]
Let the shadows fall down, let the wild wind blow
[Ad-lib] (Cowboy tears don't show, no they never show)

[Bridge]
I got a pocket full of moonlight just for you
Every silence echoing the fire in our hearts
Lost within the shadows where our destiny starts
Burning up the distance in the still of the night
Say my name softly... Saeed tonight...
[Whispered] (Saeed... Saeed...)

[Spoken]
[Spoken] (They say a rough road makes a hardened man)

[Final Chorus]
